About Us

Fresh juice is always here to serve you freshly made juices and fresh ingredients too!. FRESH juice serve walk in guest in our establisment located at Martinez Subdivision Phase 7 Dahican Mati City near the school of Davao Oriental State University. Our online website is also available from 7am to 8pm, we offer deliveries and/or bookings. For online bookings, appointments and orders, we always make sure to be responsive to all of our customers and deliver the orders on time. The estimated time of delivery is 5-15 minutes depending on the location of our customer and how many customers placed an order. Fresh juice offers various drinks but we understand that everyone have their own preferences on how they want their drinks to be mixed with, to welcome everyone we let our customers decide on what flavors they want to get and what fruits they like to put in their cup, it all depends on their decision. We always welcome our customers positively and make them feel like hanging out in their friends house. Our Goal is to put a smile on our customers face and make them worry less when drinking our juice. We want to assure them that our drinks are made from natural juices and no Food additives.
